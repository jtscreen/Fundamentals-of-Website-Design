What is a website anyway?

Well...according to the internet, a website is defined as a set of related web pages located under a single domain name, typically produced by a single person or organization.

But me? In the context of this class, I believe it is a way for people to express or advertise something or someone.


Why put things on the internet?

We put things on the internet because we feel the need to share what we do with others and it gives people easy access to a much wider range of works.


Will websites someday become obsolete?

Websites are now at the point where they will never become obsolete until the machines try to take over the world. But that will never happen 😅


What is your relationship with technology?

Very unhealthy but in the best of ways. That is all I will say about it...
